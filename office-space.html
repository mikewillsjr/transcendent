A Studio Built for Perfection, Not Compromise

Studio 1003C is not a shared coworking space with ring lights. It is a purpose-built, full-service production facility in the heart of Chelsea — one of Manhattan's most accessible creative districts. With 2,400 square feet of open floor space, 16-foot ceilings, and a permanently mounted ARRI SkyPanel lighting grid, your crew can walk in and start shooting within minutes, not hours.

The studio includes a private green room with four makeup stations, a client lounge with live monitor feed, and a dedicated post-production suite running DaVinci Resolve for on-site color grading and editing. Freight elevator access makes load-in effortless for even the largest productions.

Whether you are producing a Fortune 500 brand film, a multi-camera podcast, or a high-end fashion editorial, Studio 1003C gives you the infrastructure of a broadcast facility with the privacy of your own space.
