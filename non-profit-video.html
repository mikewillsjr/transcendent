Every Dollar Raised Starts With a Story Worth Telling

Non-profit fundraising is not about asking for money. It is about showing donors the world they can help create. We produce cinematic fundraising films that transform complex missions into visceral, emotionally compelling narratives — the kind that move audiences from empathy to action before the paddle raise even begins.

Our team has spent 18+ years embedded in the non-profit world, filming galas at The Plaza, benefit dinners at Cipriani, and scholarship ceremonies at Columbia. We understand the psychology of giving: the precise moment a story needs to land, the emotional arc that turns a table of guests into a room full of donors.

Whether you need a same-night highlight reel to drive real-time pledges or a year-long campaign film for your annual fund, we engineer every frame to maximize your fundraising outcome.

💰
🧠
Donor Psychology Storytelling
Narratives engineered to move audiences from empathy to action. We study your donor base, identify emotional triggers, and craft stories that make giving feel inevitable — not optional. Every beat is designed around proven behavioral fundraising principles.
⚡
Same-Night Highlight Reels
Live editing during galas for real-time fundraising momentum. Our on-site edit team captures key moments throughout the evening and delivers a polished highlight reel before the paddle raise — turning the energy in the room into immediate pledges.
📊
Impact Measurement
A/B tested messaging, year-over-year fundraising growth tracking. We do not just deliver a film and walk away. We measure performance across campaigns, refine emotional hooks based on donor response data, and help you raise more every single year.
